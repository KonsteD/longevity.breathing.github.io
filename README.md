# longevity.breathing.github.io
> **Master your breath, improve your health.**

A dedicated web application designed to help users learn, practice, and track their progress using the **Buteyko breathing method**—a scientifically backed technique developed by Soviet physician Dr. Konstantin Buteyko.

---

## 📖 About the Buteyko Method

The Buteyko method is a therapeutic breathing technique developed in the 1950s by Dr. Konstantin Buteyko. It focuses on retraining your breathing patterns to cure chronic hyperventilation (overbreathing). 

By practicing slow, light, and nasal breathing, the method helps restore normal carbon dioxide (**CO₂**) levels in the blood, which naturally improves oxygen delivery to tissues and organs.
